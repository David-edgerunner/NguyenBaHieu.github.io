My Artists

Since 2010
Dream pop · Baroque pop · Sad girl cinema

Lana Del Rey

She writes like someone who makes movies in their head — melancholic, cinematic, American mythology filtered through sadness and beauty. If you love Wong Kar-wai films, you already love Lana. Same melancholy, different medium.

CinematicNostalgicPoetic
02
Since 2004
Pop punk · Alternative rock · Anthemic

Paramore

Pure energy and emotional honesty — they've reinvented themselves multiple times without losing their core. The kind of band that hits differently depending on where you are in life. Perfect for gym at 6 PM.

High energyEmotionalAnthemic

Since 2018
Post-rock · Avant-garde · Maximalist

Black Country, New Road

Chaotic, literary, and completely unlike anything else. Their songs feel like reading a great novel — dense with meaning, always changing. The "weird stuff" taste you have? This is it in music form.

Avant-gardeLiteraryComplex

Indie rock · Psych rock · Dry wit

Arctic Monkeys

Alex Turner writes lyrics like a novelist — sharp, witty, loaded with imagery. Every album is a different world. A band that rewards you for paying attention, which you clearly do. Start with Humbug if you haven't.
